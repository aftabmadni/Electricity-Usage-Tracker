## ğŸ’¡ Quick Tips

### 1. **Finding Power Ratings**
- Check appliance label/sticker
- Look in user manual
- Google "[appliance name] power consumption"
- Typical ranges:
  - AC: 1000-2000W
  - Refrigerator: 100-200W
  - LED TV: 80-150W
  - Microwave: 800-1200W
  - Water Heater: 1500-3000W

### 2. **Estimating Usage Hours**
- AC: 6-10 hours (summer months)
- Refrigerator: 24 hours (always on)
- TV: 4-6 hours
- Washing Machine: 1-2 hours
- Microwave: 0.5-1 hour
- Lights: 6-8 hours

### 3. **Interpreting AI Insights**
- **Red Badge = High Priority**: Top consumer, take action!
- **Percentage Savings**: Realistic optimization goal
- **Actionable Tips**: Specific recommendations you can implement

### 4. **Optimizing Costs**
- Reduce high-consumer usage by 1-2 hours daily
- Use appliances during off-peak hours
- Replace old appliances with energy-efficient models
- Turn off when not in use (no standby mode)
